# AI for Email: Write, Summarise, and Triage 10x Faster in 2026

## Why This Matters Right Now

The average knowledge worker spends 28% of their workday managing email—that's 2.6 hours daily, or 13 hours per week lost to inbox management. With AI email assistants now achieving 95%+ accuracy in drafting contextual responses and summarising threads, you can reclaim 10+ hours weekly by automating the repetitive parts while keeping your voice authentic.

## Prerequisites

Before diving in, make sure you have:

- An email account (Gmail, Outlook, or any IMAP-compatible service)
- Access to at least one AI tool: ChatGPT Plus ($20/month), Claude Pro ($20/month), or a free API key from OpenAI/Anthropic
- 15 minutes to set up your first workflow
- Basic comfort with copy-pasting and browser extensions (no coding required for most methods)

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Choose Your AI Email Integration Method

You have three main approaches in 2026, each with different trade-offs:

**Option A: Browser Extension** (Easiest)
- Install extensions like Superhuman AI, Compose AI, or MailMaestro
- Works directly in Gmail/Outlook web interface
- Cost: $10-30/month
- Best for: Non-technical users wanting instant results

**Option B: AI Assistant + Manual Copy-Paste** (Most Flexible)
- Use ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini Advanced directly
- Copy emails in, paste responses out
- Cost: $0-20/month
- Best for: Those wanting full control and customization

**Option C: API Integration** (Most Powerful)
- Connect your email directly via Zapier, Make.com, or custom Python scripts
- Fully automated workflows
- Cost: $20-100/month depending on volume
- Best for: High email volume (100+ daily)

For this tutorial, we'll focus on **Option B** since it's free to start, works with any email provider, and teaches you transferable prompt engineering skills.
