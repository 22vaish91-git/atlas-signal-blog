Last updated: 3 May 2026

AtlasSignal provides a clear process for users to request deletion of personal data associated with connected platform integrations and account workflows.

## Request Deletion by Email

Send a request to atlassignal.ai@gmail.com with:

- Subject: Data Deletion Request
- Your account email address
- Connected platform account identifiers (if applicable)
- Optional details on what you want deleted

AtlasSignal may request basic verification to protect account security.

## Self-Service Option

You may disconnect connected accounts from platform settings (for example, YouTube, Meta platforms, X) at any time. Disconnecting stops future access for those integrations.

## Deletion Timeline

After verification, AtlasSignal processes deletion requests within 7 business days.

If legal, accounting, fraud-prevention, or compliance obligations require temporary retention of specific records, those records are restricted and retained only as required by law.

## What Gets Deleted

When applicable and permitted by law, AtlasSignal deletes:

- Stored integration tokens and related connection metadata
- User-associated workflow and publishing records
- Contact records not needed for legal compliance

## Need Help?

If you have questions about deletion status or scope, contact:

- Email: atlassignal.ai@gmail.com
- Website: https://atlassignal.in
